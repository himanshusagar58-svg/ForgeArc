Privacy Policy for website enquiries.

This policy explains what ForgeArc does with the information you share through this website or when you start a WhatsApp conversation from it.

Effective 28 June 2026

1. Scope

ForgeArc provides hospitality-focused digital systems. This Privacy Policy applies to information collected when you browse this website, submit an enquiry, call us, or use the website’s WhatsApp contact flow.

It does not replace the privacy policy for any separate website, ordering app or software system built for a client. Those client-facing systems should have their own policy and data-handling details.

2. Information we may collect

When you contact ForgeArc, you may choose to provide:

  • Your name or business name
  • Your mobile number
  • The service you are interested in
  • Your comment, requirement, outlet context or operational problem
  • Any further information you voluntarily share through WhatsApp, phone or a later project discussion

Our hosting provider may also process limited technical information needed to operate and secure the website, such as device, browser and connection logs.

3. Why we use this information

We use enquiry information to respond to you, understand the system you need, prepare or discuss a scope and quotation, provide support where a project begins, and protect the security and operation of this website.

We do not sell or rent enquiry information. We do not use it for unrelated marketing without a relevant reason or your permission.

4. WhatsApp contact flow

When you complete the contact form and select “Continue on WhatsApp,” the website prepares a message for a WhatsApp conversation with ForgeArc. The message is then handled through WhatsApp and is also subject to WhatsApp’s own terms and privacy practices.

Avoid sending sensitive information through the enquiry form or WhatsApp, including payment card details, government ID numbers, passwords or account credentials.

5. Sharing and service providers

We may share information only where necessary to operate the website, communicate with you, deliver agreed work, obtain professional advice, comply with law, or protect our rights and users. We do not share enquiry data with unrelated third parties for their own marketing.

6. Retention and security

We keep information only for as long as reasonably needed for the enquiry, any resulting project or support, record-keeping, legal obligations or dispute resolution. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no online communication or storage method can be guaranteed completely secure.

7. Your choices and requests

You may ask us to update, correct, delete or stop using your enquiry information for future follow-up, subject to applicable legal or contractual obligations. To make a request, send a WhatsApp message or call +91 95206 64381 and state that your message is a privacy request.

8. Children

This website is intended for business enquiries. We do not knowingly seek personal information from children through this contact form.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the website, services or data practices change. The effective date above shows when it was last updated.

10. Contact

For privacy questions about ForgeArc’s website enquiries, call or message ForgeArc at +91 95206 64381.
